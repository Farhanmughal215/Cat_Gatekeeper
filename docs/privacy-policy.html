Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Siamese Cat Gatekeeper is a Pomodoro productivity timer Chrome extension that blocks distracting websites during breaks. This policy explains how the extension handles your data.

Data Collection

Siamese Cat Gatekeeper does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. No information ever leaves your device.

Local Storage Only

The extension stores the following data locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local:

  • Timer settings (work/break durations, long break interval)
  • Your personal blocklist or whitelist of domain names
  • Session counts and focus scores for the current day
  • Weekly statistics (sessions and skips per day)
  • Auto-start preferences

This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

No Analytics or Tracking

The extension contains no analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind. No third-party libraries or services are used.

No Network Requests

The extension makes no external network requests. All assets (images, sounds) are bundled within the extension package.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • storage — to save your settings and session data locally.
  • alarms — to keep the timer running accurately across tab switches and browser restarts.
  • host permissions (<all_urls>) — to check whether the current page's hostname matches your personal blocklist or whitelist, and to display the break overlay when needed. No page content is ever read, modified beyond the overlay, or transmitted.
